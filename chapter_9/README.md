# Chapter 3 - Bring your own project

Having spent two days working on trains you realize you don't like trains. You would much rather work with oil & gas or
that DIY home automation project you have been meaning to get to. Screw trains, have it your way.

## Task: Fancy Application&trade;

Were you expecting a better name? No, here you have to define the name yourself.

The task here is straightforward and open. Take what you have learnt about integration testing and Testcontainers in the
workshop and apply it to your own project. Be it a work project or something you tinker with on your own, see if you can
improve the project by increasing the amount of integration testing you apply. Or maybe just add the proper database to
your unit tests?

Remember that Testcontainers has a range
of [community modules](https://testcontainers-python.readthedocs.io/en/latest/modules/index.html) that are supported by
default and the possibility of creating any custom module you might require.
